🌱 How Aloe Vera Helps Skin (Especially with Collagen)

  • Boosts Collagen Production • Research shows that taking aloe vera orally (gel) can increase collagen production in skin. • It seems to stimulate fibroblasts (the cells that make collagen + elastin), which helps skin firmness. • Also, aloe might reduce collagen breakdown by lowering certain enzymes that degrade collagen.
  • Hydration • Aloe is a humectant: it draws water into skin, helping with plumpness + elasticity. • Hydrated skin supports healthy collagen functioning — dry skin can make fine lines more visible.
  • Anti-Inflammatory + Antioxidant • Aloe has anti-inflammatory compounds which can calm irritation. • It also has antioxidants that can protect skin from damage (which helps preserve collagen).
  • Improves Elasticity • In studies, aloe supplementation improved skin elasticity. • This means combining aloe with collagen peptides might give a two-pronged effect: more building blocks (collagen) + support for your skin’s natural collagen.
